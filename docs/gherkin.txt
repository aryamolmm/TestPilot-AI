Feature: E-Commerce functionality
  As a user
  I want to be able to login, add items to cart, and checkout
  So that I can purchase products online

Scenario: Successful login
  Given I am on the login page
  When I enter valid username and password
  Then I should be logged in and redirected to the products page

Scenario: Failed login with invalid credentials
  Given I am on the login page
  When I enter invalid username and password
  Then I should see an error message indicating invalid credentials

Scenario: Add item to cart
  Given I am logged in and on the products page
  When I click the "Add to Cart" button for a product
  Then the product should be added to my cart

Scenario: Remove item from cart
  Given I am logged in and on the products page
  And I have a product in my cart
  When I click the "Remove" button for the product in my cart
  Then the product should be removed from my cart

Scenario: Successful checkout
  Given I am logged in and have a product in my cart
  When I click the "Checkout" button
  And I fill in my personal and payment details
  Then I should see a confirmation message indicating successful checkout

Scenario: Checkout with empty cart
  Given I am logged in and my cart is empty
  When I click the "Checkout" button
  Then I should see an error message indicating that my cart is empty

Scenario: Checkout with invalid payment details
  Given I am logged in and have a product in my cart
  When I click the "Checkout" button
  And I fill in invalid payment details
  Then I should see an error message indicating invalid payment details
